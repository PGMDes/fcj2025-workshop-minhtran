**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Get familiar with AWS/FCJ-2025 platforms](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Networking on AWS](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Compute VM Services on AWS](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Storage Services on AWS](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Security Services on AWS](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Database Services on AWS](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Learning knowledge on Skill Builder courses](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Learning about NLP and FastAPI](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Project deployment Sprint 01 - Research on Speech to Text and OCR models](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Project deployment Sprint 02 - Improvement and quality enhancement of models](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Project deployment Sprint 03 - Getting familiar with AWS support services and applying them to the project](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Project deployment Sprint 04 - Testing all models and error handling](1.12-week12/)
